Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
Men: BMR = (10 √ó weight in kg) + (6.25 √ó height in cm) - (5 √ó age) + 5
Women: BMR = (10 √ó weight in kg) + (6.25 √ó height in cm) - (5 √ó age) - 161
Why we use this: The Mifflin-St Jeor equation is considered more accurate than the older Harris-Benedict equation, with studies showing it predicts RMR within 10% of measured values in more individuals.
