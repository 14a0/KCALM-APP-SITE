Medical & Health Information Disclaimer

Important Notice

KCALM provides nutritional calculations and wellness insights based on peer-reviewed scientific research. This information is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

Scope of Service

KCALM is designed to help you understand and track your nutrition through:

  • Evidence-based caloric calculations using established scientific formulas
  • Macronutrient distribution based on recognized nutritional guidelines
  • Food analysis powered by advanced AI technology
  • General wellness insights derived from your dietary patterns

Medical Disclaimer

The information provided by KCALM:

  • Is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease
  • Should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice
  • May not be suitable for individuals with specific medical conditions
  • Is based on population averages and may not reflect individual needs

Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions, are pregnant or nursing, or take medications.

Individual Variations

Our calculations are estimates based on scientific research and population data. Individual nutritional needs can vary significantly based on:

  • Genetics and metabolic differences
  • Medical conditions and medications
  • Physical activity levels and type
  • Age, hormones, and life stage
  • Environmental and lifestyle factors

Accuracy & Limitations

While we strive for accuracy using validated scientific methods, please understand that:

  • Food analysis results are estimates and may vary from actual values
  • Caloric formulas represent population averages
  • Individual metabolism can differ by ±20% from calculations
  • Nutritional databases may contain inaccuracies
